# G6 generated 3D asset pipeline

The procedural character remains a temporary fallback only. New hero assets
are produced from an approved isolated concept through image-to-3D, inspected
as real glTF geometry, optimized for the browser, and integrated only after a
turntable review.

## Acceptance gate

- full silhouette is modeled on every side;
- flippers and feet are separate, riggable forms;
- no faceted or stacked-primitive construction;
- materials preserve navy feather, ivory belly, coral beak/feet, and knitted scarf;
- geometry and textures fit the mobile web budget;
- the model passes a neutral-light turntable before gameplay integration.
